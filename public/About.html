About

The Adaptive Traveler is a website conceived in traveling and the understanding that all cultures are equal but different. It is our belief that knowledge is power, education fosters acceptance of our many differences, and that despite our differences across the world we can resist hatred and cultural divisions through a better understanding of one another's way of life. We have found that through travel and cultural immersion one can better identify and understand why other cultures function in the way they do. This website aims to compile information regarding the many facets and barriers of traveling to a foreign destination so that one may not only immerse themselves better as a tourist or visitor, but also have a richer and more enjoyable experience while abroad.
